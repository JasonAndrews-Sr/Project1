Welcome to Bethany's Pie Shop

Our history, so far......

For many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicious pies at her home. In 2013, we opened our first store in Brussels(Belgium) in a cosy little street near the Grand Market. People from all over the world fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheesecakes, spicy pies.... The store became a success in no time.

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were old every day and Bethany's Pie Shop moved in to a larger building after making a € 2000 revenue. After recieving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious pies whenever you feel like it! Download our →price list
